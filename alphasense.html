Alpha-Sense 1.0:
An assistive vest to help blind and deaf with navigation

The basic idea of the helping the Blind and the Deaf to travel freely without needing another individuals help is what we aim to achieve through the ‘Affordable Vest’. This one product will help the Blind the Deaf together not needing two different things. Once the signal is received by the person, they will have a button to stop the signs indicating the message is received and well versed by the person wearing the vest.

For the Blind the Proximity sensors will help with informing the person about the distance of the obstacle, whereas for the Deaf the vibrations will help them understanding the intensity of the obstacle around them.
