Divya Drishti:
Smart Glasses for visually impaired.

We a team of 3 , Kushal jivrajni (IT), Ved Kale (E&TC), Isha Deshmukh (IT) participated in yantra innovation challenge 22 23. We participated under the theme of Inclusivity. We brainstormed a lot about topic selection and came to a conclusion of making a device which will serve visually impaired or partially visually impaired people in their daily life.

We started working on this project from September 22, our eYic journey Haulted in April 23 with semi-final results. It was truly technical journey in which we learnt more about the technical aspects of the competition and the topic itself initially things didn't went as planned, but after seniors support and hard teamwork we accomplished building building a minimum value product (MVP) of the Idea.
